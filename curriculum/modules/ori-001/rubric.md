# Rubric: status, evidence, and placement

## Rubric

The assessment is worth 100 points.

| Criterion | Points | Evidence required |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Status knowledge and audit | 25 | Correct released/planned/blocked classifications, catalog facts, learner actions, and overclaim boundaries |
| Evidence knowledge and audit | 30 | Correct labels for all four evidence types, a bounded test claim, proof scope, explicit assumption, proposal boundary, and exclusions |
| Personal path | 30 | Evidence-backed ledger, earliest-unpassed start, prerequisite order, later released steps, and first unavailable boundary |
| Clarity and traceability | 15 | Own words, readable structure, catalog snapshot, and checkable references to claimed pass evidence |
| **Total** | **100** |  |

## Critical criteria

All three critical criteria must pass:

1. **Status truth:** earn at least 20/25 for status knowledge and audit, and do
   not present a planned or blocked module as released or completed.
2. **Evidence boundary:** earn at least 24/30 for evidence knowledge and audit,
   and do not present a test, assumption, or proposal as a proof or as current
   universal behavior.
3. **Placement integrity:** earn at least 24/30 for the personal path, begin at
   the earliest assessment without passing evidence, and do not skip an
   unpassed prerequisite.

A high total cannot compensate for a failed critical criterion.

## Scoring

Assign points within each row as follows:

- **Status knowledge and audit (25):** 9 for the three knowledge scenarios, 9
  for exact catalog classifications, and 7 for correct learner actions and
  overclaim boundaries.
- **Evidence knowledge and audit (30):** 12 for the knowledge classifications,
  8 for all four packet classifications, 5 for the bounded supported claim and
  proof scope, and 5 for assumptions, proposal limits, and exclusions.
- **Personal path (30):** 10 for a truthful evidence ledger, 10 for the correct
  earliest-unpassed start, and 10 for prerequisite order and the first
  unavailable boundary.
- **Clarity and traceability (15):** 5 for complete answers in the learner's
  own words, 5 for the catalog snapshot, and 5 for checkable assessment
  references.

Pass the module only with at least 80/100 and all critical criteria.

## Feedback and retry

Feedback must name the incorrect classification, unsupported claim, missing
evidence, or path edge and connect it to an outcome ID. The learner keeps the
original submission and adds a correction note.

For a retry, the assessor supplies fresh status and evidence cases. The learner
also rebuilds the path from the current catalog rather than copying the prior
ledger. Only failed criteria need new work, but the complete revised record
must remain internally consistent.
